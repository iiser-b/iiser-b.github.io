Vision for council

Who we are

We, at the Environmental and Social Initiatives council, ESIC for short, are one of the youngest councils at IISERB. Like our name aptly suggests, we are the environmental and social arm of the SAC. We strive to raise awareness and sensitivity about a plethora of issues, ranging from Mental Health, Social Diversity, Animal Welfare, Sustainability, Climate Change, etc. The aim of this council is to help promote a healthy campus atmosphere, and to establish a peaceful coexistence with the fauna on campus.

What we do

We conduct activities like cleanliness and plantation drives, talks and workshops on the plethora of topics under our domain, collaborations with other councils on multiple events, and conduct birding sessions and resolve the issues arising from human-animal interaction on campus.
